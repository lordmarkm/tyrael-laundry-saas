The Customer List View

The Customer List View is a complete list of all customers records currently enrolled to all of your available brands.

Customers in the list are ordered as follows

  1. Customers with outstanding balance, sorted by outstanding balance amount
  2. Customers without outstanding balance, sorted by family name

Available actions

  1. Create a new customer
  2. View details about a particular customer
  3. View all job orders belonging to a particular customer
  4. Update an existing customer record
  5. Delete a customer
